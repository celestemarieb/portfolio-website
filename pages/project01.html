Project 001 : Portfolio Website

HTML & CSS


The portfolio is a publicly accessible website with a design responsive to the needs of different screen-sizes and devices.


Notable quote from this project:

Wherefore art thou javascript?

You might think I was asking here, where is my javascript?

Alas, alack - that wasn't what Juliet was asking at all

She knew where he was - he's down in the garden, she can hear him you know...

I also know where Javascript was for this project, down in the garden being a bit useless and just not that practically involved in finding a solution.


why javascript?


I asked whereforeart thou javascript, because why and how is javascript I know now after this project.

Javascript would have been real helpful for navigation, responsiveness and other fun features. No mystery anymore as to why it exists.


who needs em!


My aim for this site was minimalism and consistency to allow the content I share on this site to pop.

By working with CSS and HTML alone I was able to keep to that intention without distraction, while still able to pip in a lil bit of life and energy.


check thou out


Notable inclusions are a responsive design - adjusting to different screen-sizes, a blog and project gallery as well as a navigation bar which switches to a top left menu for mobile.

Plenty of buttons are linking you to my resume, my inbox, external and internal links.

just with a dash of HTML and sprinkle of CSS
